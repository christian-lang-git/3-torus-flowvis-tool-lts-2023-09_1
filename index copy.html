Warning

For high quality thumbnails it is recommended to deactivate panning mode by clicking into the canvas and pressing "p".

Warning

For high quality thumbnails it is recommended to wait until the progress bars at the bottom of both images disappear. This requires that the mouse is inside the canvas.

Warning

It is recommended to set the thumbnail URLs before export. If you do not know the URLs yet, e.g. because you plan to upload the thumbnails to a server where you can not choose the URLs of the uploaded images, you can leave them blank for now and:

  1. export, in order to obtain the thumbnails,
  2. upload the thumbnails,
  3. insert the URLs of the uploaded thumbnails in the fields above,
  4. export again, to obtain the correct files.
