Warnings

  • For high quality thumbnails it is recommended to deactivate panning mode by clicking into the canvas and pressing "p".
  • For high quality thumbnails it is recommended to wait until the progress bar at the bottom of the image disappears. This requires that the mouse is inside the canvas.
  • It is recommended to set a thumbnail URL before export. If you do not know the URL yet, e.g. because you plan to upload the thumbnail to a server where you can not choose the URL of the uploaded image, you can leave it blank for now, export, upload the thumbnail, insert the URL of the uploaded thumbnail and export again.
